INGREDIENTS

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 stick melted butter or ½ cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Optional: ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Optional: ½ cup nuts or chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. In a large bowl, mash the bananas using a fork or potato masher.
  2. Add the eggs, melted butter (or oil), vanilla extract, and sugar, and mix until combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder (add cinnamon here if desired).
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture and stir with a spatula until no flour is visible.
  5. Fold in nuts or chocolate chips if using.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased or lined pan.
  7. Bake for 50 minutes to an hour or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with little to no crumbs.
  8. Allow it to cool slightly before slicing.
